# A Nice Poem

The stars above shine bright at night, POEM!
The moon casts down its silver light, POEM!
The gentle breeze through willows sings, POEM!
Of all the joy that morning brings, POEM!

The rivers flow through valleys deep, POEM!
While peaceful creatures drift to sleep, POEM!
The sun will rise and greet the day, POEM!
And chase the darkest clouds away, POEM!

So let your heart be full of cheer, POEM!
For beauty blooms throughout the year, POEM!
In every leaf and every stone, POEM!
We're never truly all alone, POEM!
