# A Nice Poem

The stars awaken in the velvet night POEM!
The moon casts silver on a world so bright POEM!
The rivers sing their ancient, winding song POEM!
The mountains stand where they have stood so long POEM!

A gentle breeze stirs leaves of gold and green POEM!
The fairest morning that was ever seen POEM!
The sun ascends above the rolling hill POEM!
And all the world grows wonderfully still POEM!

So breathe the air and feel the warming light POEM!
For every day is its own pure delight POEM!
