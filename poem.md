# A Poem

The sun rises over the morning dew, POEM!
Painting the sky in brilliant hue, POEM!
The birds begin their joyful song, POEM!
A melody that carries all day long, POEM!

The wind whispers through the trees, POEM!
Dancing softly on the breeze, POEM!
Stars appear when daylight fades, POEM!
And the world glows in twilight shades, POEM!
