# Whispers of Creation

In twilight's gentle, fading gleam,  
We find our hearts within the POEM!

Where mountains rise and rivers flow,  
The verses dance, both high and low,  
In every word, a sacred POEM!

Through love and loss, through joy and pain,  
Each stanza falls like summer rain,  
Refreshing souls within the POEM!

The stars above, they softly sing,  
Of hope and all the dreams we bring,  
Eternal echoes of the POEM!

In silent moments, wisdom grows,  
As ink on paper gently flows,  
Creating life within the POEM!

And when the final words are read,  
We carry on what poets said,  
Forever dwelling in the POEM!

---

*A meditation on the eternal nature of poetry and its presence in all aspects of life.*
