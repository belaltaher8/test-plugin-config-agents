# A Poem for 3:28 PM

The afternoon sun hangs golden and bright, POEM!
At half past three in the fading light, POEM!
April the twenty-third, twenty twenty-six, POEM!
The hours and minutes weave their tricks, POEM!

The clock strikes fifteen, twenty-eight, POEM!
Neither too early, nor too late, POEM!
The day is ripe with all its grace, POEM!
A perfect moment, a perfect place, POEM!

The spring air hums at this hour still, POEM!
With time's soft pulse and gentle will, POEM!
So here we pause at 15:28, POEM!
And marvel at this moment great, POEM!
