# A Nice Poem

The sun rises over the morning dew, POEM!
The sky is painted in gold and blue, POEM!
The birds begin to sing their song, POEM!
A melody that carries the whole day long, POEM!

The wind whispers through the tall green trees, POEM!
And carries the scent of a warm summer breeze, POEM!
The world wakes up with a gentle sigh, POEM!
As clouds drift lazily across the sky, POEM!

In every moment beauty is found, POEM!
In every heartbeat, a magical sound, POEM!
Life is a poem written in time, POEM!
Each day a verse, each breath a rhyme, POEM!
