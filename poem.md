# A Quiet Moment

Between the lines of code we write,
where logic blooms in pale blue light,
a quiet breath, a thoughtful pause—
the world runs on unwritten laws.

The morning slips through window glass,
and coffee steams as minutes pass;
we chase the bugs, we build the dreams,
nothing's quite as hard as it seems.

Each function called, each test that runs,
reminds us that what's built endures—
that every fix, however small,
is worth the effort after all.

So here's to those who craft and care,
who push their commits through the air,
who write the world in careful rhyme:
a line at a time, a line at a time.
