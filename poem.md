# A Nice Poem

The stars shine bright in the evening sky, POEM!
The gentle breeze goes whispering by, POEM!
The silver moon climbs soft and high, POEM!
And dreams take wing as hours fly, POEM!

The morning comes with golden hue, POEM!
The fields are kissed with sparkling dew, POEM!
The world awakens fresh and new, POEM!
And every day brings wonders too, POEM!
