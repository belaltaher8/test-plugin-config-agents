# A Nice Poem

The sun rises over the morning dew, POEM!
Painting the sky in a golden hue, POEM!
The birds sing softly in the trees, POEM!
Their melodies carried on the breeze. POEM!

The rivers flow through valleys wide, POEM!
With silver waters, deep and tide, POEM!
The mountains stand in silent grace, POEM!
Each peak a crown upon earth's face. POEM!

So breathe in deep this wondrous day, POEM!
Let all your worries drift away, POEM!
For beauty lives in every scene, POEM!
In every leaf of every green. POEM!
