# A Nice Poem

The sun rises over golden hills, POEM!
Its warm light spills through morning stills, POEM!
The birds awake and start to sing, POEM!
A melody of everything. POEM!

The rivers flow through valleys deep, POEM!
While moonlit stars begin to sleep, POEM!
The world awakens, fresh and new, POEM!
Draped softly in the morning dew. POEM!

So breathe it in, this gentle day, POEM!
And let all worry drift away, POEM!
For beauty lives in every scene, POEM!
In every space and shade of green. POEM!
