# A Poem for 5:53 PM

The clock reads five-fifty-three,
The afternoon begins to flee,
A golden hour draws gently near,
As dusk prepares to soon appear.

The sun hangs low in amber haze,
The final act of daylight's blaze,
Shadows stretch across the floor,
A quiet knock at evening's door.

The world slows down, the rush subsides,
The day releases what it hides,
A breath, a pause, a moment free—
The peace of five-fifty-three.

*Written at 17:53 UTC, April 24, 2026*
